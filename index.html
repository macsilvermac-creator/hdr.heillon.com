THE PIPELINE

Every HDR follows the Z-J-M-H-K protocol.

Z
ZONE
Define operational domain and boundaries
→ zoneId, boundaries[]
J
JURISDICTION
Verify cryptographic authority and mandate chain
→ authorityId, mandateHash, scope[]
M
MANDATE
Declare intention before execution
→ intentionHash, declaredAt, nloTarget
H
HEILLON SEAL
Execute and seal with SHA-256 hash
→ executionHash, sealedAt, chainPosition
K
KNOWLEDGE
Certify NLO level and generate audit record
→ nloLevel, auditTrail, reportHash
